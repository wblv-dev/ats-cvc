ATS-CVC
Gap Analysis

CV gap analysis, not a CV writer. Identifies what's missing, what's undersold, and what a hiring manager will probe on in interview.

↗ Use the tool ↗ GitHub Repository

What it does

ATS-CVC applies an evidence-based framework to your CV and a specific job description, producing five structured outputs every time, for any role in any industry:

Role Fit AssessmentCan you genuinely do this job? Honest percentage fit against the essential requirements.
Seniority CalibrationDoes your CV read at the right level? Task-focused vs outcome-focused, scope of impact, language of ownership.
Keyword & Positioning GapWhat is missing, and what are you calling something different to the JD?
Expand These PointsWhat is present but doing insufficient work for this specific role?
Interview Risk FlagsWhat will a hiring manager probe on, and are you ready?

How it works

  1. Choose your AI provider. Mistral is free (phone number only, no card). OpenAI, Gemini, and Anthropic require a minimum spend paid directly to that company.
  2. Get your API key from your chosen provider and paste it in. Your key goes directly to them, not to this tool.
  3. Upload your CV as a PDF and paste the job description.
  4. Confirm your name and role title — extracted automatically, editable.
  5. Run the analysis. Results render in seconds.
  6. Save as PDF if you want a clean report to work from.

Privacy

This tool has no backend, no server, and no database. Your CV, job description, and API key travel directly from your browser to your chosen AI provider. Nothing passes through any intermediate server. Nothing is stored, logged, or retained after you close the tab.
  • CV is parsed locally in your browser using PDF.js — never sent to any server
  • API key is held in memory and cleared on tab close
  • Results are rendered client-side, never transmitted or stored
  • No analytics, no tracking, no cookies
  • I make no money from this tool and see no data from it

The framework

AI is the engine that applies the ATS-CVC Framework. The framework is the product. It's an evidence-based methodology built on real hiring data — recruiter eye-tracking studies, ATS parse failure rates, and structured gap analysis principles. The five analytical outputs are consistent regardless of which AI provider you choose.

The full methodology, evidence base, and prompt engineering documentation is available in the METHODOLOGY.md on GitHub.
