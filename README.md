# ATS-CVC — CV Gap Analysis Tool

I was doing research into hiring in the UK, CV best practice, design choices, what ATS systems can and can't read etc - I found out that most of the time a CV tailored directly to a job desc will out perform others and receive better feedback from hiring managers. I took all that research and made a small framework & a site which can give you notes about your CV when comparing it to a job desc, it won't write your CV for you (that's kinda stupid) but it'll tell you where you are weak, what you've nailed etc.
